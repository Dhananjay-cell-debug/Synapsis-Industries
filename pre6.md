# PRE-6 — Archived Questionnaire Questions (Phase 1)

> These were the original 6 questions used in the Phase 1 questionnaire.
> Archived on 2026-03-27.
> Reason: These are pre-sales questions — designed for convincing a client, not for gathering project intelligence.
> They were replaced with discovery-focused questions better suited for post-election stage.

---

1. If nothing changes and this problem is still unsolved 6 months from now — what does your business look like? What has it cost you?

2. What is the measurable price your business pays every week this goes unsolved — in time, money, or missed opportunity?

3. What solutions have you already tried — tools, agencies, people, or DIY? What results did you get from each?

4. If we agree on scope, timeline, and investment tomorrow — what is the one thing that could still stop this from moving forward?

5. Six months after this is built and running — describe exactly what your business looks like. What's different, specifically?

6. When it comes to approving this project and releasing the budget — who is that person, and is it you?
